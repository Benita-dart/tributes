A TRIBUTE TO KENNETH ERWIN HAGGIN

The father of the "Word of Faith" Movement

PERSONAL LIFE

Kenneth E. Hagin was born on the 20th of August, 1917. As a young child, he suffered from a terminal blood disease (deformed heart) that left him bedridden at age 15. As his illness grew worse, his parents, Jess Hagin and Lillie Viola had began making funeral arrangements to bid their son a peaceful farewell. It was in April 1933, he died and was resurrected on three occasions, each time seeing the horrors of hell and then returning to life. This experience was the turning point of his life as he received Jesus as his Lord and savior. Thereafter Jesus completely healed him of the terminal disease with a scripture that eventually birthed the Rhema Bible Church.


MINISTRY

In 1949, Hagin began an itinerant ministry as a Bible teacher and evangelist. At the same time he received full admission into the Full Gospel Business Men's Fellowship International. He was also a part of a major healing revival in U.S that took place in the the 40s and 50s. Kenneth E. Hagin Evangelistic Association was incorporated in 1963 and became the the corner stone for numerous media outreaches and ministries which includes:

  • The faith library publication RHEMA praise
  • Faith Seminar of The Air
  • The Word of Faith
  • Rhema Correspondent Bible School
  • Rhema Prayer And Healing Center

In his lifetime, Hagin authored several books that have continued to inspire and train both young and mature believers in the Christian faith. Some popular publications include: The Believer's Authority, Bible Faith Study Course, How You Can Be Led by the Spirit of God, The Name of Jesus, Growing up Spiritually & The Triumphant Church.
